## Portfolio tour sequence (for interviews)

When a recruiter asks "walk me through your portfolio", do it in this order:

1. **Lead with ContinuityAI** (3 min) — the $600M Netflix context is the strongest hook. Open the UI, click through a shot analysis, show the severity classification.
2. **Show one that matches the role's stack** (3 min) — RAG for Disney, Diffusion for Netflix Inkubator, Rec Engine for Paramount.
3. **Show the landing page briefly** (1 min) — to signal breadth without getting into every project.
4. **Stop and ask them what they want to see deeper.** Don't bulldoze.

Total demo time: 7 minutes. Leave room for questions.

## Red flags to screen out

Don't waste applications on roles that won't value your portfolio:
- Jobs that emphasize certifications over demonstrated work
- Jobs with 10+ required tools in the description (they don't know what they need)
- Jobs without a named hiring manager after 2 rounds of Google
- Jobs below $140K base in LA — your portfolio justifies more
